The project aimed to determine the satisfaction level of passengers of Maven Airline. The data was 
collected from a survey of the airline's passengers.

PROBLEM - Determine the satisfaction of passengers with the Airline's services

SOLUTION
- Almost 60% of the passengers were dissatisfied with the Airliine's services
- Female Passengers were more dissatisfied than male passengers
- Passengers betweeen in their early 20s to late 30s were the most dissatisfied while passengers
  above the age of 40 showed a higher level of satisfaction
- More passengers in Economy class were satisfied with the services than passengers in Business 
  class
- Passengers that traveled for business showed a higher level of satisfaction than passengers 
  that traveled for personal reasons
- The passengers were most dissatisfied with the In-flight Wifi and Ease of Online booking
